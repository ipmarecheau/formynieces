## Summary

- **Section I (mechanics) edges:** ~20
- **Section II (comprehension + poetry + graphic) edges:** ~30
- **Writing edges (in + out):** ~14
- **Total ELA edges:** ~64

Apex nodes (require the most): 72 (Organisation/Coherence), 86 (Poetry evaluation),
90 (Graphic evaluation), 79 (Text evaluation) — the hardest anchors sit here.
Root nodes (required by the most): 52 (basic spelling), 58 (basic punctuation),
62 (Parts of Speech), 73 (Main Idea) — the foundations.

Combined with Math (~77), the full dense graph is **~141 edges**.

**Once you approve ELA, the next build step is the seeder** that loads all approved edges
(Math + ELA) into `module_prerequisites`, plus a structural test asserting: every edge
references real module ids, no self-loops, and the graph is acyclic (a cycle would break
mastery inference).
